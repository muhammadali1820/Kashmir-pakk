Muzaffarabad

Muzaffarabad, the capital of Azad Jammu and Kashmir, is known for its stunning beauty. Famous spots include:

  • Pir Chinasi
  • Red Fort
  • Shaheed Gali

Neelum Valley

Neelum Valley is a paradise for nature lovers. Must-visit places include:

  • Keran
  • Sharda
  • Arang Kel

Rawalakot

Rawalakot, the "Pearl Valley," is surrounded by lush green hills. Popular attractions are:

  • Banjosa Lake
  • Toli Pir
  • Poonch River

Bagh

Bagh is known for its scenic landscapes and waterfalls. Explore:

  • Bagh Fort
  • Dhirkot
  • Lohar Gali

Mirpur

Mirpur offers a mix of history and modern development. Key places include:

  • Mangla Dam
  • Ramkot Fort
  • Jari Kas Park

Kotli

Kotli is known for its peaceful environment and natural beauty. Don't miss:

  • Haji Peer Pass
  • Teenda Dam
  • Sarsawa
